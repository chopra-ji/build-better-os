# Mission — Creator Engine

---

## Mission Statement

The Creator Engine exists to close the gap between a technically correct script and a performant Instagram Reel.

A script that passes the Script Department's quality gates is complete, accurate, and on-brief. It is not automatically optimized for attention, retention, or social behavior. Those are different problems. The Creator Engine solves them.

---

## The Gap This Engine Fills

The Script Department produces scripts that are:
- Faithful to the Creative Brief
- Structurally sound
- Factually accurate
- Correctly formatted for production

What a script from the Script Department is not guaranteed to be:
- Optimized for the first 3 seconds of an Instagram scroll
- Structured to resist drop-off at the moments Instagram audiences typically leave
- Written in the register that performs on social platforms
- Designed to provoke comments, shares, or saves
- Compressed to the 45–60 second window Instagram rewards

The Creator Engine reviews the script against all of these criteria and produces an optimized version. The ideas and core message remain unchanged. The delivery, language, structure, and timing are what get refined.

---

## Definition of Success

The Creator Engine succeeds when:

1. Every script that enters the pipeline exits as a production-ready Instagram Reel script — 45–60 seconds, platform-native, and engineered for engagement.
2. No script exits the pipeline with an unresolved quality failure.
3. Every optimization made is traceable to the specific criterion it addresses.
4. The original message, tone, and intent of the Creative Brief are preserved through every optimization.
5. The creator can read the Final Production Script and know exactly what to record — without additional interpretation.

---

## What This Engine Is Not Responsible For

- **Ideation.** The Creator Engine does not suggest topics, angles, or creative directions. That is the Creative Department's function.
- **Brief creation.** The engine does not write or modify Creative Briefs.
- **First drafts.** The engine does not write scripts from scratch. It receives completed scripts.
- **Distribution.** The engine's responsibility ends at Final Production Script delivery. Publishing and scheduling are outside its scope.
- **Performance reporting.** The engine is not responsible for post-publish analytics or performance tracking.

---

## Strategic Alignment

The Creator Engine is the operational mechanism for Build Better's core competitive advantage: producing content that is not only right, but built to perform. Every piece of content that passes through this engine carries a quality guarantee that no piece produced without it can match.

Over time, the engine's scoring history and optimization patterns become institutional knowledge — a record of what works on Instagram for Build Better's specific audience and voice. That record belongs to the organization, not any individual creator.
