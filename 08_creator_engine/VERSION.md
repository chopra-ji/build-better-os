**Current Version: 1.0.0**

---

## Versioning Schema

`MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`

| Change Type | Increment |
|---|---|
| Fundamental change to mission, pipeline structure, or scoring model | MAJOR |
| Addition or removal of a stage, dimension, or optimization protocol | MINOR |
| Corrections, clarifications, or wording improvements to existing content | PATCH |

Version history is in `CHANGELOG.md`.
