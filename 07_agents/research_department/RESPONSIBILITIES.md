## Explicitly Out of Scope

| Area | Owned By | Why It Is Out of Scope |
|---|---|---|
| Writing editorial content | Content Department | Research produces structured knowledge documents, not editorial articles, scripts, or posts. The output is the intelligence, not the final content. |
| Deciding editorial direction | Workflow Department | Research informs decisions; it does not make them. Topic selection, angle, and editorial stance are owned by editorial leadership. |
| Publishing decisions | Publishing Department | Research provides input on channel and format trends, but scheduling, platform selection, and distribution are outside research scope. |
| Business strategy decisions | Leadership | Competitive Analyses and Technology Reports surface findings; strategic decisions about Build Better's direction are made by leadership, not the research function. |
| Managing external vendor or creator relationships | Relevant department | Research may review a tool or platform but does not negotiate, contract, or manage relationships with external parties. |
| Domains outside the ten monitored | Designated specialist or ad hoc | Research outside the ten defined domains requires an explicit scope extension decision from the Platform Director. |

## Responsibility Changes

When a responsibility is added to or removed from this department:

1. Update this file with the new or removed responsibility.
2. Update `INPUTS.md` and `OUTPUTS.md` if the change affects the department's interface contract.
3. Update `WORKFLOW.md` if the change affects operating flow.
4. Update `KNOWLEDGE.md` if the change adds or removes a monitored domain.
5. Increment version in `VERSION.md` (MINOR for additions/removals).
6. Add an entry to `CHANGELOG.md`.
7. Notify the Platform Director and all affected downstream departments.
