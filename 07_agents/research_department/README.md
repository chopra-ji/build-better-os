# Research Department — Department Overview

## What This Department Does

The Research Department is the intelligence function of Build Better OS. It monitors ten strategic domains — Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Business, Marketing, Psychology, Creator Economy, Startups, Books, Frameworks, and Productivity — and converts what it finds into structured documents that other departments use to make decisions, develop content, and stay ahead of their audiences.

This department does not create content. It creates the knowledge that makes great content possible.

---

## Why This Department Exists

Build Better operates at the intersection of media and intelligence. Every editorial decision, every content brief, every publishing strategy depends on having an accurate, current, synthesized view of the world. Without the Research Department, that view is fragmented across individual people's reading habits, informal conversations, and outdated assumptions. This department centralizes, systematizes, and structures that intelligence so the entire organization can act on it.

## Output Types at a Glance

| Output | Purpose | Typical Length | Standard-Tier SLA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Research Brief | Answer a specific research question | 500–1,000 words | 48 hours |
| Trend Report | Synthesize a movement across a domain | 800–1,500 words | 5 business days |
| Competitive Analysis | Structured comparison of entities in a space | 1,000–2,000 words | 7 business days |
| Idea Report | Synthesized ideation from research signals | 600–1,200 words | 4 business days |
| Technology Report | Deep dive on a specific technology topic | 1,200–2,500 words | 10 business days |
| Framework Summary | Distillation of a thinking framework | 400–800 words | 3 business days |
| Tool Review | Structured evaluation of a tool or platform | 600–1,000 words | 3 business days |
| Knowledge Update | Vault update triggered by new information | Vault entry format | 2 business days |

*Expedited and urgent turnarounds are defined in `WORKFLOW.md` → Timing and SLAs.*
