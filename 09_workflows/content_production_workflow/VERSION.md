## Versioning Schema

`MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`

| Change Type | Increment |
|---|---|
| Addition or removal of a workflow stage; fundamental change to escalation structure | MAJOR |
| Addition or removal of a gate criterion; new output field; new escalation trigger | MINOR |
| Corrections, clarifications, or wording improvements | PATCH |

Version history is in `CHANGELOG.md`.
